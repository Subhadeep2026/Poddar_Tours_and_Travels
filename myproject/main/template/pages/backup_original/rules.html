Rules & Regulations

A. Booking Policy

  • Booking amount per person is Rs. 5,000/-. Balance payment must be completed at least 30 days before the journey.
  • Bus seats are allotted strictly as per booking sequence.
  • Solo guest bookings are generally not confirmed due to twin-sharing accommodation. Single room may be provided on payment of additional charges, subject to availability.
  • Hotels may provide double or triple sharing rooms. In triple sharing, a floor mattress may be provided.
  • No room preference is allowed, even for early bookings.
  • Child Discount (without bed):
    • 5 to below 12 years – 15% discount
    • 3 to below 5 years – 50% discount
  • Infant Charges (below 3 years):
    • Up to 9 days – Rs. 1,195/-
    • Up to 13 days – Rs. 1,395/-
    • Up to 16 days – Rs. 1,695/-
  • Tour price does not include entry fees to sightseeing spots or monuments.
  • GST @ 5% will be applicable as per Government norms.

B. Rail & Air Tickets

  • Tour cost does not include rail or air fare unless mentioned.
  • Railway tickets will be arranged subject to availability. Berth or class preference cannot be guaranteed.
  • Air tickets will be arranged on request at actual cost.
  • Refund of cancelled air tickets depends solely on airline policies.

C. Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation before 45 days – Rs. 1,500/- per person.
  • Cancellation within 45 days:
    • 45–30 days: 5% + Rs. 1,500/-
    • 29–25 days: 10% + Rs. 1,500/-
    • 24–20 days: 15% + Rs. 1,500/-
    • 19–10 days: 28% + Rs. 1,500/-
    • 9–5 days: 33% + Rs. 1,500/-
    • 4–3 days: 50% + Rs. 1,500/-
  • No refund within 72 hours of departure.
  • No refund for unutilized portion of tour.

D. General Terms

  • Poddar Tours & Travels acts only as a facilitator and does not own transport or hotels.
  • The company is not responsible for delays or changes due to unavoidable circumstances.
  • Each passenger may carry one suitcase (22”) and one handbag.
  • Passengers are responsible for their own belongings.
  • Washing clothes in hotels is strictly prohibited.
